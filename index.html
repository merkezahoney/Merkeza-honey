Golden Adey Flower Honey

When Ethiopian New Year comes in September the country’s fields are filled with yellow Adey flowers. Honey harvested in October to December is the result of these flowers. This honey, like the flowers, is yellowish/golden in color. Merkeza provides this honey variety largely during these months. Now is the time to order the Merkeza golden honey and taste the liquid gold of Ethiopia!

FLEXIBILITY

Merkeza honey’s clients range from small markets, restaurants, gift shops to big honey distributers and factories spanning the world. So, our international supply depends on the client’s demand. Merkeza honey accepts orders starting from 50 Kg to tons of kilos. After entering a legal business contract, we deliver to international clients in 10-45 business days.
