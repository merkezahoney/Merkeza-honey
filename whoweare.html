ORIGIN

Haimanot is a young woman from Addis Ababa with big vision. Being a city girl, she didn’t have prior knowledge of nor experience in agriculture. But she had deep passion for agriculture, especially bee keeping, so she taught herself and bravely started a bee keeping business. This happened two years ago. Slowly she increased the number of beehives and continued to provide her honey produce nationwide.


UNITY

In her line of work, she met another young woman with similar goals; her name is Selamawit. Selamawit runs a ***** business. These two young women have combined their knowledge and experience to successfully increase their honey supply and get excellent results.

TODAY

Today, the two young women continue to practice beekeeping using a considerable number of beehives. They have extended their reach and built a strong collaboration with local beekeepers all around Ethiopia. They are ensuring quality standard compliance of the honey produce collected from these farmers and providing it to the market under the brand Merkeza. Merkeza honey is a quality assured excellent Ethiopian honey offered to local and international markets.

VISION

Merkeza aims to make Ethiopian honey beloved around the world much like Ethiopian coffee. Merkeza honey is a pure and organic honey that complies with international quality standards. That’s why we call Merkeza honey Ethiopia’s liquid gold.
